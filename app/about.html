Spirit Level

from Workshop 14 Limited

Spirit Level is a webapp that makes use of your devices sensors to give you a freely available and tactile application.

It has been developed by Workshop 14 to demonstrate the capabilities available to modern webapps that would normally be associated with apps available only through app stores. In development our next project is the more ambitious globe which we hope to be releasing soon.

We hope you like Spirit Level. If so please checkout globe and share them with others who might enjoy them.
